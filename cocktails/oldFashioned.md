# Old Fashioned

## Ingredients
- 2 oz bourbon
- 0.25 oz simple syrup
- 1 dash angostura bitters
- 1 orange slice
- 2 Luxardo cherries


## Instructions

Add bourbon and simple syrup to an old fashioned glass and stir.

Add the orange slice to the glass and muddle it.

Add ice cube and Luxardo cherries and serve.
